@last100bills

What is this?

@last100bills is a Twitter bot that posts daily breakdowns of the 100 most recent bills introduced in the US government. It uses GovTrack.us API to collect the data.

Label Description
Introduced This bill is in the first stage of the legislative process. It will typically be considered by committee next before it is possibly sent on to the House or Senate as a whole.
Passed House (Senate next) This bill passed in the House and goes to the Senate next for consideration.
Passed House & Senate (President next) This resolution was passed by Congress and goes to the President next.
Agreed To (Concurrent Resolution) This concurrent resolution was agreed to by both chambers of Congress. That is the end of the legislative process for concurrent resolutions. They do not have the force of law.
Agreed To (Simple Resolution) This simple resolution was agreed to on. That is the end of the legislative process for a simple resolution.
Ordered Reported The committees assigned to this bill sent it to the House or Senate as a whole for consideration.
